## Notable Conviction Examples (2023)

### Oman Fisheries Company
- **Position**: Marketing and Sales Manager
- **Violations**: 
  - Accepted USD 5,400 bribe
  - Accepted USD 794 from foreign company
  - Attempted to obtain USD 7,115 from another foreign company
  - Laundered USD 6,194 at bank outside Oman
- **Convictions**:
  - Money laundering (felony)
  - Misuse of position
  - Violation of Foreigners Residency Law
- **Sentence**: 
  - 10 years imprisonment + OMR 50,000 fine (1st conviction)
  - 3 years imprisonment + OMR 1,000 fine (2nd conviction)
  - 1 year imprisonment + OMR 500 fine (3rd conviction)
  - 1 month imprisonment + OMR 200 fine (4th conviction)
  - Confiscation of funds
  - Removal from office
  - Permanent deportation

## Audit Findings (2023)

### Natural Shrimp Aquaculture Company
- **Loss**: OMR 3.7 million (revenue loss Jan 2022 - May 2023)
- **Issue**: Non-achievement of harvest targets, below-target selling prices
- **Action**: Nursery ponds commissioned, survival rate reached 90% vs 65% target

### Oman Environmental Services (be'ah)
- **Loss**: OMR 151,000 (penalty reductions May 2021 - April 2023)
- **Issue**: Decline in solid waste management contractor performance (17% decrease)
- **Additional**: OMR 40,000 non-collected contribution (2019-2022)
- **Action**: KPIs amended, amount collected September 2023

## Royal Assignment Tasks (2023)

### Omanization Review
- Reviewed Omanization indicators in private sector
- Evaluated succession plans in government companies
- Proposals submitted to His Majesty including:
  - Cease recruitment of non-Omanis for Omanized professions
  - Increase Omanization in technical/supervisory roles
  - Match job titles between company and Ministry of Labour records
  - Require succession plans for non-Omani positions

### National Product Purchase Review
- Audited 165 units commitment to national products
- Proposals to establish electronic platform for local products database
- Recommendations for standardized procurement forms
