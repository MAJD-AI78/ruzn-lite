# Law on Protection of Public Funds and Avoidance of Conflict of Interest
## قانون حماية المال العام وتجنب تضارب المصالح - المرسوم السلطاني رقم 112/2011

### Key Definitions (Article 1 - المادة 1)

**وحدات الجهاز الإداري للدولة (State Administrative Units):**
- Council of Ministers (مجلس الوزراء)
- Ministries and their subordinate administrative and technical bodies (الوزارات وما يتبعها)
- Specialized councils (المجالس المتخصصة)
- Public institutions and bodies (الهيئات والمؤسسات العامة)
- Any administrative unit deriving authority from the State (أي وحدة إدارية تستمد سلطاتها من الدولة)

**المال العام (Public Funds):**
- All real estate or movable property owned publicly or privately by the State
- Property of any State unit
- Companies where government holds more than 40% (الشركات التي تساهم فيها الحكومة بنسبة تزيد على 40%)
- Private funds managed/supervised by those units (Waqf, Zakat, orphan funds, palaces)

**المسؤول الحكومي (Government Official):**
- Any person holding a government position (permanent or temporary)
- Members of the State Administrative Council (with or without compensation)
- Government representatives in companies
- Employees of companies where government owns 40%+ of capital

**المنفعة (Benefit):**
- Any compensation received by a government official (direct or indirect)

## Core Prohibitions

### Article 4 - المادة 4: Protection of Public Funds
- Public funds are **inviolable** (للأموال العامة حرمتها)
- Must be preserved (يجب المحافظة عليها)
- No disposal except according to law
- No seizure or infringement
- Cannot be acquired or gain any rights through prescription
- Any infringement is **void** and must be removed administratively

### Article 5 - المادة 5: Duty to Report
Government officials **MUST**:
- Prevent misuse of public funds (يحول دون إساءة استعمال المال العام)
- **Immediately report** any violations related to public funds (يبلغ الجهات المختصة فوراً)

### Article 6 - المادة 6: Conflict of Interest - Supervision
Government officials **CANNOT** deal with any company or institution in which they have an interest (direct or indirect) if that entity is under their supervision.

### Article 7 - المادة 7: Abuse of Position
**PROHIBITED:**
- Using position or work to achieve personal benefit or benefit for others
- Exploiting influence to facilitate others obtaining benefits
- Preferential treatment
- Any action that harms public funds or wastes them

### Article 8 - المادة 8: Acting as Agent/Representative
**PROHIBITED:**
- Acting as agent, representative, or guarantor for any company/institution connected to their work
- Mediation activities to help companies obtain government approval

### Article 9 - المادة 9: Personal Use of Public Funds
**PROHIBITED:**
- Using public funds for personal purposes or purposes other than designated

### Article 10 - المادة 10: Private Sector Work
**PROHIBITED:**
- Combining government position with private sector work (permanent or temporary)
- Exception: With proper authorization

### Article 11 - المادة 11: Ownership Stakes
**PROHIBITED:**
- Government officials (or their minor children) holding shares in companies connected to their work
- Exception: Shares acquired before this law took effect

### Article 12 - المادة 12: Financial Disclosure
Government officials **MUST** submit annual financial disclosure to OSAI including:
- All movable and immovable assets owned by them, spouse, and minor children
- Source of these assets
- Declarations are **confidential** - only accessible with OSAI President's approval

### Article 13 - المادة 13: Confidentiality
Government officials **MUST NOT** disclose secrets learned through their position, even after leaving office.

## Penalties (العقوبات)

| Article | Violation | Penalty |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Article 14** | Crimes specified in this law | Penalties in Omani Penal Code or other laws |
| **Article 15** | Violating Articles 4, 5, 8, 9, 10, 12 | **6 months to 2 years imprisonment** |
| **Article 16** | Violating Article 7 (Abuse of position) | **1 to 3 years imprisonment** |
| **Article 17** | Violating Article 11 (Ownership stakes) | **3 months to 1 year imprisonment** |
| **Article 18** | Violating Articles 15, 16, 17 | **Dismissal from position** + **Confiscation of all funds** received in violation |
