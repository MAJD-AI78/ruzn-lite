## Classification of Public Fund Cases

Based on the 2024 Annual Report, OSAI addresses the following violation types:

1. **Use of position to realize an advantage** (استغلال المنصب لتحقيق منفعة)
2. **Acceptance of bribery** to perform an act contrary to job duties (قبول الرشوة)
3. **Information forgery** (تزوير المعلومات)
4. **Forgery in official instruments** (التزوير في المحررات الرسمية)
5. **Embezzlement of public fund** (اختلاس المال العام)
6. **Failure to perform job duties** (الإخلال بواجبات الوظيفة)
7. **Transgression of public fund** (التعدي على المال العام)

## Real Conviction Examples (2024)

### Case 1: Environment Authority (Al Wusta Governorate)
**Violations:**
- Using positions to realize advantage for themselves and others
- Profiting from jobs by exceeding powers
- Permitting transport of filling materials without approval
- Non-issuance of environmental violations

**Convictions:**
- Accepting bribe to perform task contrary to job duties
- Forgery in official instrument
- Using forged document with knowledge
- Money laundering
- Breach of job duties
- Misuse of public fund

**Penalties:**
- Imprisonment: 6 months to 1 year
- Removal from public office
- Permanent prohibition from assuming public office
- Fine: OMR 71,226
- One accused: Permanent deportation

---

### Case 2: Telecommunication Company
**Violations:**
- Abusing position to realize personal advantage
- Embezzling public funds
- Using forged instruments
- Conducting fraudulent guarantee refund transactions

**Convictions:**
- Embezzlement by forgery
- Forgery in official instruments
- Using forged instrument with knowledge
- Using IT means to commit information forgery
- Using position to realize personal advantage
- Public fund transgression
- Using public office to realize advantage

**Penalties:**
- 2-year imprisonment
- Fine: OMR 111,925
- Restitution: OMR 111,925
- Removal from office
- Permanent prohibition from public office

---

### Case 3: Oman Investment Authority
**Violations:**
- Abusing position in Contract and Procurement Department
- Embezzling public funds related to travel invoices
- Suspected forgery in employee ticket quotations
- Collusion with travel agency owned by relative
- Manipulating airline ticket prices

**Convictions:**
- Embezzlement by forgery
- Using IT means to commit information forgery crime
- Information forgery in government data
- Deliberately failing to perform job duties
- Using job to realize advantage for self and others
- Transgressing public fund
- Money laundering

**Penalties:**
- 5-year imprisonment
- Fine: OMR 407,334
- Removal from office
- Permanent prohibition from public office
- Restitution: OMR 407,334 (joint with another accused)
- Additional: 5-year imprisonment for money laundering + OMR 50,000 fine
- Co-accused (travel company owner): Same charges + money laundering
- Third accused: 1-year imprisonment + OMR 10,000 fine

---

### Case 4: Oman Air
**Violations:**
- Using authorities in booking system (call center)
- Changing and upgrading travel tickets
- Misappropriating service fees

**Penalties:**
- 1-year imprisonment
- Removal from office
- Permanent prohibition from public office

---

### Case 5: Sohar Municipality
**Violations:**
- Using position and profiting from job
- Issuing administrative violations against private sector establishments
- Collecting violation amounts in cash without depositing

**Convictions:**
- Misuse of position
- Violation of duties to obtain benefit
- Using position to realize advantage

**Penalties:**
